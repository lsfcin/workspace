# [ craft | research | year ] LIH.DD — research lab

Laboratório de Inteligência Híbrida para Design Distribuído. A research lab at the university. The mission: design computational mechanisms to reorganize social behavior toward collectivity. Systems as means, not ends. Disruption via human organization in symbiotic collaboration with technology. Attacking root problems: income concentration, individualism, lack of critical thinking.

Structural pillars:
- **collectivity**: systems that mediate human cooperation
- **scale**: designed for disruptive scale — continuous induction of collectivity
- **hybrid intelligence**: symbiotic co-evolution human/MAS
- **distributed design**: intelligent systems operating in continuous regime (24/7), beyond episodic participatory design

This lab stays in the university. Synergy with the instituto (outside university) but different operational contexts.
