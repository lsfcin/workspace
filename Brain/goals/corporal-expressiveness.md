# [ spiritual | body | dream ] corporal expressiveness

Learning body language. Learning the voice. Expressing in a way I can visualize: calm, connected, secure, receptive, true, protagonist of my thoughts, courageous, aware of mission — yet light with myself and the world. Learning singing, liking my own voice, controlling it. Not performance — presence through the body.

>**signals**  
meaningful · optional · indifferent

>**fears**  
*what · being seen as awkward, or hearing a version of self that doesn't match the internal image  
when · when performing, recording, or speaking in front of others  
why · gap between internal experience and external expression — the inside feels one way, the outside lands differently  
how · avoiding situations that require exposure of voice or body — silence over discomfort*

>**analysis**  
"Parar de me esconder" / "me permitir desagradar" — the mantras already name it. This goal sits at the intersection of fear of judgment and body awareness. The indifferent engagement signal might be a protection mechanism rather than genuine disinterest — the description is written with real desire. The ease-start is designed to create a private feedback loop before any audience exists.

## selected next achievement
    [record-self] record 3 minutes of yourself talking about something you care about

**ease-start**  
Record yourself talking for 3 minutes about something you genuinely care about. Don't listen immediately — put the recording away for an hour. Then listen once. The goal isn't critique — it's just to meet yourself as others hear you.
