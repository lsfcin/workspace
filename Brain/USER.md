## Patterns to Know

- **Finishing problem**: over-refines, loses interest, overwhelmed by new things. `closure` and `ease-start` address this directly.
- **Fear mapping**: high-stakes goals carry completion anxiety. `fears` block = real data — read when diagnosing stalls. `analysis` = synthesis layer on top.
- **Fear of judgement**: to be considered as not intelligent, or not serious enough, be labeled as incompetent. 
- **Fear of leadership**: I fear a lot to have people "under my wings". The core fear is to fall middle way, fail on the consistency / discipline and let things fall apart.
- **Honor armor**: tends to carry others' expectations as obligations — guessing what others expect and trying to satisfy all of it. This generates exhaustion and avoidance. "Tirar o peso da armadura do cavaleiro da honra." Relevant when goals feel heavy with relational obligation.
- **Dopamine seeking**: phone/distraction use is partly a dopamine loop. "Conter a busca incessante por dopamina." Connects to smartphone-addiction goal and sleep procrastination pattern.
- **Hiding and permission to displease**: "parar de me esconder / me permitir desagradar" — relevant to corporal expressiveness and instituto goals. Public commitment is a recurring block.
- **Internal timing**: almost all timing self-imposed. Treat `anchor` dates as aspirations unless explicitly flagged hard. `tolerance` usually reveals missing = manageable.
- **Motivation over obligation**: impact × engagement drives 80% of energy. `essential` requirement goals get 20% — enough to satisfy, not primary focus.
